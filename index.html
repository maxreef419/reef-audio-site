About

REEF Audio is a boutique audio production studio specializing in original music, sound design and sonic branding for advertising, branded content and screen productions.

Founded by composer and producer Max Kouznichenkov , REEF combines over twenty years of advertising experience with a production approach shaped by both creative and agency-side expertise.

We work as a trusted music partner for brands, agencies and production companies — helping shape, produce and deliver the musical side of projects from the earliest creative discussions through final delivery.

At the core of every project is a commitment to musical quality. Whether creating an original score, a brand sonic identity or a production-driven campaign soundtrack, we focus on music that is distinctive, emotionally engaging and crafted to stand the test of time.

Capabilities

Bespoke music for advertising, branded content and screen productions.

Vocal casting, choirs, live musicians, orchestration and production management for projects that require a broader production approach.

Distinctive sonic identities and audio assets designed to strengthen brand recognition.

Music-led sound design where every sonic element is crafted to work with the score, not compete against it.
